About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in Amsterdam, Netherlands. I work at Booking.com with some of the most talented folks I've ever met, creating ways to empower people to experience the world.

Before joining Booking.com, I worked at Konga.com - An e-commerce company in Nigeria, mainly focused on building out the payment vertical of the company, and before that I worked at Andela.

My past experiences cut across working with small to large companies, co-founding a startup, and helping to build other startups.

Previously, I have had to work across multiple roles including - product design, print design and web development but these days my interests lie more in strategy, design-thinking, experimentation, user research, usability testing and front-end implementation.

I have a background in graphic design, architecture and urban planning.

In my spare time, I love to play video games, create art, and take pictures.

I'm currently unavailable for work, but if you want to chat, you can contact me.
