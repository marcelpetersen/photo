About

I'm Ernest. Currently based in Amsterdam, working at Booking.com as a UX designer.

I was previously at Konga, and before that I was at Andela.

Most of my work revolves around creating and rethinking digital experiences.

My expertise includes; User Interface Design, Web Development, Rapid Prototyping, User Research, and Print Design.

I have a background in graphic design, architecture and urban planning.

In the past I've had the pleasure of working at startups, design agencies, and worked with small and large scale businesses from around the world.

I've also tried and sucked at launching a successful startup of my own.

I'm currently unavailable for work, but if you just want to chat, you can contact me.
